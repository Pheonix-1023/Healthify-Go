Dry Fruits

Dry fruits are fruits that have been dried to remove most of their water content. Some popular dry fruits include raisins, almonds, cashews, and dates.

Benefits of Dry Fruits

  • High in nutrients like vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.
  • Good for heart health, digestion, and boosting energy.
  • Convenient snack option with a long shelf life.

Juices

Juices are liquid extracts obtained from fruits or vegetables. They are packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

Popular Types of Juices

  • Orange Juice - Rich in Vitamin C.
  • Apple Juice - Good for heart health.
  • Carrot Juice - Great for eye health.
  • Pineapple Juice - Supports digestion and has anti-inflammatory properties.

Fresh Fruits

Fresh fruits are naturally occurring foods that come directly from plants. They are rich in vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants.

Health Benefits of Fresh Fruits

  • Boost immunity with Vitamin C-rich fruits like oranges.
  • Promote digestion with fiber-rich fruits like apples.
  • Hydrate the body with water-rich fruits like watermelon.

Healthy Snacks

Snacks are small meals or food items typically consumed between regular meals. Healthy snacks can keep your energy levels stable and your body nourished.

Popular Healthy Snacks

  • Greek Yogurt with Berries
  • Hummus with Carrot and Celery Sticks
  • Nut Mix with Almonds, Walnuts, and Cashews
  • Whole Wheat Crackers with Cheese

Healthy Foods

Healthy foods are rich in essential nutrients that help maintain health and prevent disease. They include whole grains, lean proteins, fruits, vegetables, and healthy fats.

Top Healthy Foods

  • Leafy Greens (Spinach, Kale)
  • Whole Grains (Quinoa, Brown Rice)
  • Lean Proteins (Chicken, Fish, Tofu)
  • Avocados (Rich in healthy fats)
